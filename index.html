Meet Sarathi,
Your AI Campus Assistant

A multilingual, document-grounded chatbot you can inject into any website with a single script tag. Powered by Rasa, LangChain, and Gemini.

💉

Injectable Widget

One script tag, one init call — works on any website without conflicts. Fully scoped CSS.

🔍

RAG-Grounded Answers

Retrieves from your document corpus via ChromaDB embeddings, then generates with Gemini LLM.

🌐

Multilingual

Handles English, Hindi, and Hinglish queries natively with BGE-M3 multilingual embeddings.

Quick Integration

Add Sarathi to your website in two lines.

<!-- 1. Add the widget script -->
<script src="widget/sarathi-widget.js"></script>

<!-- 2. Initialize with your config -->
<script>
  SarathiWidget.init({
    serverUrl: "http://localhost:5005/webhooks/rest/webhook",
    botName:   "Sarathi",
    subtitle:  "AI Campus Assistant",
    greeting:  "Hello! Ask me about admissions, fees, or exams.",
    starterPrompts: [
      "Admission process?",
      "Scholarships available?",
      "Fee deadlines?",
      "Exam schedule?"
    ]
  });
</script>
